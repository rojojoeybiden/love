January 01, 2024

What a start for the new year. My love of my love with me on the beach since April of last year. We ate food that our families cooked the day prior, we finally swam together for such a long time, we laughed together while we were in the waters, we looked at the stars in the sky, and we love. It's honestly an awesome first day of 2024. Hope the year gives more of these vibes wherein were together happily in the moment.
